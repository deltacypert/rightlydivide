What is it?

Rightly Divide is a user-friendly Bible Reading App that facilitiates daily scripture study and personal refelection. Think of it as a Biblical journal. All of your observations in one place.

What can you do?

Rightly Divide allows for you to log observations on your Bible reading and revist it later, making Bible study engaging and organized. Observations can be searched by type, chapter and verse, or date created.
